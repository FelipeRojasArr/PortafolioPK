  • Research project about thermal inversion in Chihuahua

    On my first year of college, I was invited to participate in a research of thermal inversion phenomenon present in Chihuahua. The research was leaded by Cimav. Altough the main objective was to analyse and get data for future researchs, with the data we were collecting we made a new branch in the project by starting to publish information to the public to warn them about possible danger when the effect was strong enough. Since this phenomenon has a high correlation with pollution, people doing activities outside on early hours of the morning (especially activities outdoors) could be in risk of damaging internal organs due pollution gases retained. By publishing information, we could alert people to take some cautions.
